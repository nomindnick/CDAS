The Contract is changed as follows:

DESCRIPTION OF CHANGE:
Electrical system upgrade to include replacement of existing electrical panel in the main mechanical room with a new 800A service panel to accommodate increased capacity requirements for the new HVAC system. The existing panel was found to be insufficient for the new loads and does not meet current code requirements.

JUSTIFICATION:
During demolition, it was discovered that the existing electrical panel is rated for 400A service, which is insufficient for the new HVAC equipment specified in the contract documents. The panel also shows signs of deterioration and does not meet current electrical code requirements for the intended loads.
