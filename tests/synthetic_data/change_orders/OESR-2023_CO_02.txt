The Contract is changed as follows:

DESCRIPTION OF CHANGE:
Additional structural reinforcement for HVAC equipment support in the mechanical room ceiling. The existing structure requires additional steel members to properly support the weight of the new rooftop HVAC units.

JUSTIFICATION:
During inspection of the mechanical room ceiling structure, it was determined that additional reinforcement is required to safely support the new HVAC units. The original structural drawings did not accurately reflect the as-built conditions.
