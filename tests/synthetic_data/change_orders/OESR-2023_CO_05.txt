The Contract is changed as follows:

DESCRIPTION OF CHANGE:
Structural steel reinforcement for mechanical equipment mounting. Install supplemental structural steel members in the mechanical room to properly support rooftop equipment connections.

JUSTIFICATION:
Based on structural engineer's review of the as-built conditions, additional reinforcement is required at the connection points between the mechanical room and the new rooftop equipment. The existing steel members do not provide adequate support for the equipment load paths.
